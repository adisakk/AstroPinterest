AstroPinterest

A system like Pinterest but will take you out of this planet. You can look into deep space,

save the picture you like, find the history of your favorite one back to 10 years, and many more.

NASA APOD:

Astronomy Picture of the Day or APOD. Discover the cosmos! Each day a different image or

photograph of our fascinating universe is featured, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer.
